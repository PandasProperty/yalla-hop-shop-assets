Privacy Policy

Yalla Hop · Last updated: 24 July 2026

Yalla Hop ("we", "us") makes and sells printable digital products (planners, trackers, logbooks and coloring books) through our Etsy shop. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our website presence and our official social media accounts on Facebook and Instagram.

Who we are

Yalla Hop is a small independent digital-products shop. You can reach our shop at etsy.com/shop/YallaHop.

What information we process

We use a Meta (Facebook) application solely to publish our own marketing content (posts, images and videos) to our own Yalla Hop Facebook Page and Instagram account, and to read aggregate performance statistics (reach, views, likes) about that content. This application does not collect, request, or store personal information from visitors or followers, and it offers no login or sign-up for third parties.

When you interact with our Page or posts (for example by liking, following or commenting), Facebook and Instagram process that activity under their own privacy policies. We may see public, aggregate engagement information that those platforms provide, but we do not gather additional personal data about you.

How we use information

Any aggregate engagement statistics we receive are used only to understand which content is helpful and to improve what we publish. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for their own marketing.

Data retention

Because our application does not collect personal data from the public, there is no personal data for us to retain on your behalf. Aggregate statistics provided by the platforms are kept only as long as useful for planning our content.

Your rights & data deletion

You can ask us about, or request deletion of, any information you believe we hold about you. See our Data Deletion Instructions, or contact us using the details below.

Third-party platforms

Our content is distributed through Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest and Etsy. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version.
