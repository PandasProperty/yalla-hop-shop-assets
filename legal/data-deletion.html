User Data Deletion Instructions

Yalla Hop · Last updated: 24 July 2026

Yalla Hop uses a Meta (Facebook) application only to publish our own marketing content to our own Facebook Page and Instagram account and to read aggregate performance statistics about that content. The application does not collect, request, or store personal data from visitors or followers.

Because we do not build a profile of you or store your personal information, there is normally no personal data for us to delete. If you nevertheless believe we hold any information about you and you would like it removed, you can request deletion at any time:

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to anda.nenu@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the Facebook or Instagram username you used to interact with us, so we can locate any related information.
  3. We will confirm receipt and delete any information associated with your request within 30 days.

You can also remove Yalla Hop's access to your Facebook information at any time from your Facebook account: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites.

Contact

Questions about data deletion: anda.nenu@gmail.com.
